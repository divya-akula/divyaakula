## What's Inside

This handbook is written for practitioners — not theorists. It addresses the operational gap that many organizations face: knowing that responsible AI matters, but not knowing *how* to implement it day-to-day.

**Chapters span the full lifecycle:**

From establishing an AI intake process and conducting risk assessments, through building accountability frameworks for deployment, to setting up monitoring, incident response protocols, and continuous improvement loops — this book gives you actionable guidance at every stage.
